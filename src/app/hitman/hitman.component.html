Text Based Hitman

Check out the source on github!

Project Summary

I was inspired to make this project after working with the UNIX command line in my Introduction to Unix course at Poly. I wanted to make some sort of text based program that allowed the user to enter commands with tab autocomplete. I decided to make a game because I love programming games and it seemed like a lot of fun. This project started as a text-based choose your own adventure game, but once I built some rooms and allowed the player to move between them I decided a hitman style game with full procedurally generated levels would be more fun.

This project was tons of fun because it combined language processing and game development which are two of my favorite things.

Gameplay

Enter commands to scope out the area.

Find clues that lead you to your targets and complete your mission.

A fully procedurally generated world provides a unique experience every time you play.

Commands

You can press TAB to autocomplete the commands and their arguments. For example, hitting TAB after typing "pickup kni" will fill in "pickup knife". This makes playing the game much easier. The commandline also supports a history function so you can use the up and down arrows to view and repeat previous commands.

The following commands are supported:

amuse (person) - tell a joke to lift a person's spirits, it may backfire

apply (poisonName) to (itemName) - poison a weapon or consumable item

approach (person) - get in close quarters to a person, giving them a chance to react. Required for melee attacks

attack (person) - attack a person with the equipped weapon

capture (person) - capture a terrified person to get an extra life. Only "Fearful" people can be made terrified.

cheerup (person) - increase a person's happiness

chokeout (person) - render a person unconscious

command (person) (pickup/goto/attack/stop) (target) - command an ai to take an action

complement (person) - give a complement to increase happiness and attraction

consume (item) - eat or drink an item to regain health

describe (area/item/person) (_/itemName/personName) - display the description for (area/item/person)

disguise (person) - disguise yourself as a worker by taking their clothes

dishearten (person) - decrease a person's happiness

drop (item) - drop the specified item

escape (item) - make your escape after completing all of the objectives

equip (item) - make a weapon ready to use

followme (person) - ask a person to follow you. Only works if they trust you

give (item) to (person) - give an item to a person

goto (room) - move to the specified room if possible

forcegoto (room) - force your way into a room. Ignores locked doors but alerts the guards in the next room.

help (command) - display information on the command (arg) or lists commands if (arg) is empty

inquire (personStat/personInfo/items) person

inspect (clue) - reveal information about a clue

intimidate (person) - Intimidate a person to make them afraid of you. Lower there resistance to your influence.

leaveme (person) - Causes a person to stop following you

peek (room) - reveal items and people in an adjacent room

pickup (item) - add item in the area to inventory

place (item) in (item) - place an item into another item if possible

punch (person) - hit a person with your fists

quit () - exit the game

romance (person) - romance a person and generate a new life. Other person must have full attraction.

save () - save the game

scout (room) - investigate a location that the current room overlooks

search (area) - reveal items and people in a room

seduce (person) - increase attraction by a lot. hance to fail based on morality.

survey () - reveal buildings in an area

talk (person) - have a conversation with a person. gives a piece of information and raises their trust.

takefrom (person/container) (item) - take an item from a person

unequip (item) - hide a weapon

unlock (door) - unlock a door if you have the right key

view (items/time/stats personName/my stats/my companion/objectives/visited rooms) - display (inventory/time/stats for a person)

wait - Do nothing and allow the ai to take a turn

Guards

Watch out for guards. They will be suscpicious of your actions, especially if one of your targets is nearby. Find a disguise to fool them and be sure to use silent weapons to avoid attracting unwanted attention.

Items

You start out with only your trusty silenced pistol and a syringe. To gain the upperhand, search the environment for useful items like food, keys, and more weapons. The more items you collect, the more successful you will be in completing your mission.

NPCs

The people around you can be your best friends or your worst enemies. Befriend them and they will give you valuable information and even follow your orders. Slight them and they will attack and alert the guards.

If an NPC trusts you enough, you can use them as backup in case you get incapicated during your mission.

Gameplay Examples

Search the area to see what items and people are in the current area.

Escape items are required to complete the game. Complete the mission objectives and get to the exit!

Key items allow you to open doors with the matching type

Container items hold other items

Consumable items restore health when consumed. When given to NPCs it increases their trust in you. High trust means the NPC will follow your commands and even fight with you if their trust is high enough.

Search containers in the current area.

 Survey the surrounding area. Peek into an adjacent area to see what items and people are there.

Attempt to goto an adjacent area. If there are guards with the "Warning" status level they will attack on sight. The game asks for verification before completing this action if that is the case.

Moving to another area with an NPC following you will make them come with you.

 View items that are in your inventory and equip a weapon. Goto an adjacent area and search to see the items and people in that area. Attack someone with the equipped weapon. The ammo count decreases by one after each shot and will prevent you from attacking if it drops to 0. Any guards in the area will be alerted to you once you attack. Using a loud weapon will alert guards in the adjacent areas.

Disguise as someone that has a job in the area by taking their clothes.

The following jobs are possible for the NPCs: Barkeep, Chef, Groundskeeper, Janitor, Maid, Civilian, Guard, and Target.
